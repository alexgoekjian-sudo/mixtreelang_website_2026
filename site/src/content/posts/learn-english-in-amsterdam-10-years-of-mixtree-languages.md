<h1>MixTree Languages: 10 years of community-driven English education in Amsterdam</h1>
This year marks the 10th anniversary of <strong><a href="https://mixtreelang.nl/about-mixtree-languages/">MixTree Languages</a></strong>, a non-profit English language school in Amsterdam that has played a key role in building community and empowering international residents through quality English language education.

Since its opening in 2014, MixTree has evolved from a small cultural association into a thriving educational hub, assisting thousands of newcomers in their integration into Amsterdam's vibrant life.
