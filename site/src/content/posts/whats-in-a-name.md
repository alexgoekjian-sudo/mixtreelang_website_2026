<h1 dir="ltr">Why the name MixTree Languages? Here's the story...</h1>
<h4 dir="ltr">The Beginnings of MixTree Languages</h4>
<p dir="ltr">Founded in 2014, MixTree Languages is a <strong>non-profit English language school </strong>in the heart of <strong>Amsterdam</strong> designed to help adults from all over the world improve and expand their English.</p>
<p dir="ltr">Our name reflects a simple yet powerful idea: <strong>just like a tree, we grow by combining strong roots, flexible branches, and flourishing fruits. And, just like a tree, language skills grow day by day, nourished by knowledge, practice, effort, and conversation.</strong></p>

<h2 dir="ltr"><img class="size-medium wp-image-33267 alignright" src="/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/MTL_LOGO_512-300x300.png" alt="" width="300" height="300" />Our Logo</h2>
<p dir="ltr">Our logo—a tree in constant growth—symbolizes this concept. Every student, teacher, and learning moment represents a new leaf, branch, or fruit, enriching our shared journey.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong>The Roots: Commitment to the Community</strong></p>
<p dir="ltr">MixTree is deeply rooted in its commitment to Amsterdam's <strong>international community</strong>. Beyond teaching English, we organize <strong>Language</strong> <strong>Exchange Meetups</strong> where students can socialise and practice speaking in a safe and welcoming space.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong>The Trunk: Our Educational Mission</strong></p>
<p dir="ltr">We provide <strong>high-quality English courses</strong> in a friendly, <strong>multicultural setting</strong>, for all levels. Our teaching is based on the theories of social learning. This means that we believe language is learnt not only by acquiring vocabulary and grammar, but by being able to communicate and share ideas, experiences, and emotions and also to learn from others. We also believe that <strong>in-person, face-to-face learning</strong> is invaluable, as it allows <strong>real-time interaction</strong>, immediate feedback, and a stronger sense of community.</p>

<p dir="ltr"><strong>The Leaves: Student Success Stories</strong></p>
<p dir="ltr">Our students' achievements reflect our impact. By providing a dynamic and supportive environment, we help them improve their <strong>language skills faster </strong>and integrate into Amsterdam's culture. Their progress is our greatest reward.</p>

<h3 dir="ltr">What Makes MixTree Languages Different?</h3>
<p dir="ltr">Our <strong>collaborative/social learning</strong> approach sets us apart. Language isn't just learned through 'input' but also through interaction. We emphasize connection, encouraging students to share ideas, both in class and through WhatsApp groups or cultural events.</p>
<p dir="ltr">This approach aligns with <strong>the Zone of Proximal Development (ZPD)</strong>:</p>
<p dir="ltr">"...the ZPD is the difference between what a person can achieve when acting alone and what the same person can accomplish when acting with support from someone else..." (Lantolf, 2000)</p>
<p dir="ltr">By providing <strong>a supportive learning environment</strong>, we help students step out of their comfort zones and grow in their language skills. With experienced, CELTA-certified teachers, MixTree is more than a school—it's a community-driven path to language mastery.</p>

<h3 dir="ltr">In Summary</h3>
<p dir="ltr">MixTree Languages is like a growing tree, rooted on inclusivity, accessibility, quality, and flexibility. With the energy of <strong>Amsterdam's expat community</strong>, our English school keeps expanding, providing a friendly and safe space where students can improve their <strong>English skills</strong> and connect with others in a warm, supportive environment.</p>
